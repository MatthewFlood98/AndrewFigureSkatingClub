Programs

We will be taking all precautions with our club to ensure it will be a safe and fun extra curricular activity for your children during Covid-19. Follow us on Facebook for updates.

Our skating club runs various programs. We offer:

CANSKATE

The program is geared to beginners of all ages…children, adults, newcomers to Canada and athletes with a disability (AWAD). Whether you are looking to improve basic skating skills for figure skating, hockey, ringette or speed skating, or wish to skate for recreation, fitness or fun, CanSkate will get you there.


POWERSKATING

Focuses on skating technique through balance, control and agility skills, to enhance skating ability.


FIGURE SKATING

This program is for those skaters who start out in canskate and get to about stage 4 but would like to go more into the actual figure skating side of skating which is more into the jumps and spins etc.
